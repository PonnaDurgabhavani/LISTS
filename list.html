TOMATA RICE RECEIPE

Lets see how to prepare tomato rice receipe

INGREDIENTS

  • Boiled rice
  • onions
  • mirchi
  • oil
  • tomatoes
  • turmeric powder
  • salt
  • water
  • chilli powder

PROCEDURE TO MAKE TOMATO RICE RECEIPE

  1. turn on the flame
  2. pour oil into the pan and heat the oil
  3. cut yhe onions, mirchi, tomatoes and fry them in oil until it cooks
  4. add salt and turmeric powder according to the taste
  5. after cooked add boiled rice and have your rice
